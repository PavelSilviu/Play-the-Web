Persona - Sarah Thompson

Background

Sarah is a 32-year-old stay-at-home parent with two young children. She has a background in graphic design but put her career on hold to focus on raising her kids. Sarah is eager to continue learning and staying mentally active while managing her household responsibilities.

Main Points

  • Enjoys creative pursuits and has a passion for art and design.
  • Limited free time due to childcare responsibilities and household chores.
  • Values convenience and flexibility in learning options.
  • Seeks engaging and interactive activities to make learning enjoyable.

Goals

  1. Continued personal growth: Sarah wants to pursue her passion for art and design, improve her skills, and stay mentally stimulated.
  2. Time management: She wants to make the most of her limited free time and find a learning solution that fits into her daily routine.
  3. Balance between learning and family life: Sarah aims to integrate her learning activities seamlessly with her responsibilities as a parent and homemaker.

Frustrations and Pain Points

  • Limited availability of time: Sarah finds it challenging to dedicate long hours to learning due to her busy schedule.
  • Lack of structure and motivation: Without a clear learning framework, Sarah struggles to stay motivated and often feels overwhelmed.
  • Difficulty finding appropriate learning resources: Sarah faces challenges in discovering suitable art and design learning materials that cater to her skill level and interests.

Detailed Description

Sarah is a creative and motivated individual who has taken a break from her professional career to focus on raising her children. She has a strong desire to continue growing personally and maintaining her artistic skills. However, her daily routine revolves around managing household chores and taking care of her kids, leaving her with limited free time.

Sarah values convenience and flexibility in her learning options. She is seeking an application that can provide engaging and interactive activities that align with her artistic interests. Additionally, she desires a structured learning approach that helps her stay motivated and progress in her chosen field.

Scenarios

  1. Scenario 1 - Creative Break

    During her children's nap time, Sarah decides to take a break and indulge in her artistic pursuits. She opens the learning application on her tablet and engages in short, interactive art tutorials that allow her to practice her skills and try new techniques in a fun and convenient way.

  2. Scenario 2 - Learning on the Go

    Sarah is often on the go, shuttling her children between activities. While waiting for her child's dance class to finish, she opens the learning app on her smartphone. She engages in quick art quizzes and challenges that keep her mentally stimulated and allow her to make progress in her artistic journey, even during busy moments.
